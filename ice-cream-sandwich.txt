Ingredients:
 - 1 pint premium ice cream (flavor of your choice)
 - 16 large gingerbread cookies (about 4 in diameter)

Directions:
 1. Soften ice cream slightly by micowaving for 10 seconds.

 2. Spread 1/4 c. ice cream on 8 of the cookies.
 
 3. Place a second cookie on top of each.

 4. Freeze until ready to eat.
